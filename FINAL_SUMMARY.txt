                            KEY FEATURES

ISSUE #565: Withdrawal Scheduling Validation
  Functions:
    - schedule_withdrawal(vault_id, caller, timestamp, amount)
    - validate_withdrawal_schedule(vault_id, timestamp, amount)
  
  Features:
    - Prevents overlapping withdrawals within 1-hour window
    - Conflict detection mechanism
    - Event emission for tracking

ISSUE #566: Withdrawal Limits by Time
  Functions:
    - set_withdrawal_limits(vault_id, caller, daily, weekly, monthly)
    - get_withdrawal_limits(vault_id)
    - check_withdrawal_limits(vault_id, amount)
  
  Features:
    - Daily limit: 24 hours (86,400 seconds)
    - Weekly limit: 7 days (604,800 seconds)
    - Monthly limit: 30 days (2,592,000 seconds)
    - Automatic period resets
    - Optional (backward compatible)

ISSUE #567: Withdrawal Destination Whitelist
  Functions:
    - add_whitelist_address(vault_id, caller, address, label)
    - remove_whitelist_address(vault_id, caller, address)
    - get_whitelist(vault_id)
    - is_whitelisted(vault_id, address)
  
  Features:
    - Restrict withdrawals to whitelisted addresses
    - Optional (no whitelist = all allowed)
    - Audit trail with timestamps and labels
    - Backward compatible

ISSUE #568: Withdrawal Reversal
  Functions:
    - reverse_withdrawal(vault_id, caller, withdrawal_id)
    - get_withdrawal_reversal(vault_id, withdrawal_id)
    - record_withdrawal_for_reversal(vault_id, amount, grace_period)
  
  Features:
    - 24-hour grace period (86,400 seconds)
    - Automatic recording of all withdrawals
    - Auto-incremented withdrawal IDs per vault
    - Prevents double-reversal
    - Restores funds to vault balance
